Problem
42% - 44% of the kids in our city have obesity (body mass index - BMI = 30, or higher), according to a recent study made by the "Comisión de Salud y Educación"

It is a problem that needs to stop! Children with overweight problems tax our resources now and in the future. These children could develop diabaties and heart diseases at a later time.

The state of Jalisco and the city of Guadalajara needs to have strong healthy children !
